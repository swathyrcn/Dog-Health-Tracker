General Tips for Your Dog’s Health

Regular Grooming

Brush your dog's coat every 2–3 days to prevent matting and remove loose fur.

Healthy Diet

Provide a balanced diet rich in proteins, healthy fats, and essential vitamins.

Dental Care

Brush your dog's teeth 2–3 times a week using a dog-specific toothbrush and toothpaste.

Exercise

Ensure daily physical activity suited to your dog's breed, such as a 30-minute walk or play session.

Hydration

Keep fresh and clean water available for your dog at all times to stay hydrated.

Parasite Control

Regularly check for ticks and fleas, especially after outdoor activities, and use vet-approved treatments.
